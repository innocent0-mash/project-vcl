About VCL

Varanasi Coders League (VCL) is a student-run hackathon aimed at fostering innovation, collaboration, and a spirit of friendly competition among the students of Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith (MGKVP). We believe in the power of code to solve real-world problems and aim to provide a platform for students to showcase their skills, learn from their peers, and connect with industry professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Any student currently enrolled in any course at MGKVP can participate.

What is the team size?

Teams can consist of 2 to 4 members.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in VCL is completely free.
