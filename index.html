About VCL

Varanasi Coders League (VCL) is a student-run hackathon aimed at fostering innovation, collaboration, and a spirit of friendly competition among the students of Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith (MGKVP).

We believe in the power of code to solve real-world problems and provide a platform for students to showcase their skills, learn, and connect with industry professionals.
